Call Reports

The Call Tracking Metrics report includes an overview of all call statistics, a graph detailing the total number of calls by hour, day or month with number of unique calls, Total calls, calls by source and number. Call reports provides call tracking statistics of online and traditional advertising to help marketers analyze ad and call agent performance. To Check the Graphical representation of Call Reports follow the below steps:

  • Go to the Dashboard․
  • Click on Call Reports in menu․
  • Click on Report View․

Report View:

To check the Report of any publisher or campaign follows the steps.

  • Select you publisher or Campaign.

  • Select the date for which you want to view the Graph

  • Click on the SUBMIT button.

  • And you will be able to check the whole graph as per the requirement.

  • In business this graphical representation helps you to increase the functionality of your system.

Reporting:

The Report View will be highlighted by colors, so that you can easily identify the reports.

  • Sky-blue shows here the Total Call.

  • Dark Blue shows here the Total Unique Calls.

  • Yellow color shows here the Total Unique Answered Calls.

  • Purple shows AHT (Average Handle Time).

You can Check Hourly status by click Hourly Button. The calls by hourly graph provide visual trends of calls and AHT by hour for the report period. An interactive hover feature enables viewing of statistic for every hour.

Similarly to View the weekly Status, click on the weekly button.

Similarly to View the Monthly Status, click on the Monthly button

Report Dates:

Select a Report Date range from the Report Options calendar tool or select a Custom Date Range in the top right toolbar.
