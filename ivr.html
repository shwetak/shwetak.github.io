IVR:

AlivePBX provides a well-designed software system that can help to increase customer satisfaction & improve contact center operations .The Digital Transformation best practices, including real time routing, voice recognition and Interactive voice response solutions & machine learning powered automation optimization, to achieve the contact center.
IVR telephony is a technology to make your call automation software work for customers, not against them. An IVR system accepts a combination of voice telephone input & touch-tone keypad selection and provides the appropriate response in the form of Voice, callback, email and the other contact methods

To check the IVR’s details click on the ’IVR’ & you can see three categories in IVR.

  • IVR-Music
  • Manage IVR
  • Add IVR

IVR Music:

You can Change Your IVR-Music as per your business requirement.

  • Go to on the user for that you wish to modify the music

  • Click on the Edit icon.

  • In ‘Music File Upload’ you can upload new music from your list.

  • Click on the ‘Edit IVR-Music’ button.

  • And you are successfully changed your user’s IVR-music.

  • If you do not want to modify you music, click on the ‘Cancel’ button.

  • If you want to delete the user from the IVR, click on the ‘Delete’ button.

  • You can also add the Users in your IVR List with music.

Manage IVR:

  • Go to the link.

  • Click on the Manage IVR.

  • Here you can see the list of IVR’s with all detail.

  • Name shows the assigned IVR’s name

  • Directdial would be disable always

  • Description defines the IVR description that is assigned to the IVR.

ADD IVR:

  • To Edit the IVR Click on the edit icon which you can see in the User’s list.

  • You can see the below image ‘ADD IVR’ page on the screen.

  • You can directly add IVR by clicking on the IVR > Add IVR option in menu.

  • Enter the Name

  • Enter the description of IVR.

  • Enter the Announcement (the opening music when call starts to connect).

  • Select the Publisher’s name.

  • Click on the ‘Save and Continue’ Button.

  • You will see the next page on the screen .

  • Enter the selection that is a number where can route the calls according to your number.

  • Enter Buyer Number.

  • Click on the Save and continue button.

  • Set it Live
