Manage Queue:

Queue is a concept used in inbound call centers. Call Queue make it easier for callers to access telephony services, while the call center is relieved due to ACD (Automatic Call distribution) Queue.

AlivePBX define here Queue is a list of extensions by which you can divert the call and a strategy for how to handle the queue and divide the calls between members or Agents. Queue Consist of members that answer the Queue call are Agents. When call divert to the right Queue firstly Music played while waiting in the Queue.

Queue Management is important to maintain and improvement the service level of a call centers.

To Manage Queue follows below steps

  • Go to Dashboard.

  • Click on the Manage users.

  • Click on the Manage Queue.

Here, you can see all the details like Name, Email, Contact no., Address, created Date, Buffer time, price per Call, Queue status, Action.

You can edit the user details by clicking on Edit icon in Actions.

  • If you want to change one of the detail of any user.

  • Click on that particular user.

  • Change the details by clicking edit Queue.

If you do not want to change, click on the cancel button.

Manage Number:

To manage numbers follow the steps.

  • Click on the icon that is given in Action.

  • Add new Queue number

  • To delete any queue number from number list.

  • Click on the Delete icon.
