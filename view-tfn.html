TFN

Toll Free Number is particularly common for customers-service calling. A TFN service has traditionally provided customers and others with a free and convenient way to contact business. Such numbers allow callers to reach businesses and individuals out of the area without being charged a long distance fee for the call.

Ever thought about the business applications of Toll free numbers?

  1. Customer can reach business anytime anywhere, improving customer satisfaction.

  2. Provide vanity number that is easily accessible for customers.

  3. Cost effective as customers don’t pay anything for the call.

  4. Used in marketing campaigns as an effective tool.

  5. Create good brand recognition for the business.

  6. Help in expending the customer base.

  7. Set separate rules for your business numbers

  8. Enable call recordings for inbound & outbound calls

  9. Allow incoming calls to show your business number instead of caller’s number to know it’s a business call.

There are 6 categories in TFN(S) to check .

You can check TFN number, Publisher name, TFN price, status & Action.

Status:AlivePBX define available TFN & Used TFN.

  • If Status is ‘Available’ it means you can assign the TFN for other publisher, currently not assigned with anyone. When you will assign to a publisher it would be ‘used’ status in list.
  • If Status is used it means TFN is used by other publisher.

Action: In action you can see the three icons in the list, where you can Edit the List and Delete the list.
