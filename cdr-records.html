Call Detail Records

Call Detail Record is a data record produced by a telephone exchange that documents in details of a telecommunication transaction that passes through that device. CDR Report typically shows data broken down either by user or by phone number. A CDR report generated for a single user can show specific metrics, such as call volume and minute. You Can use the data to verify monitor employee phone activity and Keep track of when, and how your phone system is used.

To check the CDR reports it is used to navigate and use the AlivePBX reporting interface. It contains all the filters that we need to show your data in real time.

The Reporting interface is broken down into 3 sections.

➢CDR Reports
  1. Average handling time(AHT)
  2. Total Calls
  3. Total Unique Calls
  4. Total Unique answered Calls
  5. Qualified Calls
  6. Buyer Earing
  7. Buyer Spending
  8. Net Earning

Publisher, The CDR Reports works as a summary of Call Detail that allows you to observe average Caller handling times. You can find your data by Selecting Buyer,
Campaign, Date and Filter option. Select one of the publisher, buyer or campaign & now you can check the events accordingly.

To check the events, CDR reports have 8 Categories.

  • Average handling time(AHT)

    It represents the average duration of an interaction with a customer, usually measured from customer’s initiation of the call and covering all talk time and hold time until the end of the call.

  • Total Calls

    Number of calls connected to the IVR.

  • Total Unique Calls

    Number of calls which has unique number and customer’s query resolved by single agent.

  • Total Unique answered Calls

    Unique count of calls answered by at least one agent.

  • Qualified Calls

    Number of calls connected to the Agent.

  • Buyer Earning
  • Buyer Spending
  • Net Earning
