Account Setup (How To Create your first Campaign)

Create Campaign:In AlivePBX platform all calls route through campaign, Whenever you start create your campaign Follow the below Steps .

Create your campaign in just 4 steps.

  1. Start your Campaign

  2. Publisher & Routing

  3. Add a Target

  4. Set it Live

STEP 1- START YOUR CAMPAIGN
  1. Click on Campaign & select Create new campaign. Enter the below details.
  • Enter your Campaign name. “It will be define to route at the AlivePBX platform & Reporting interface to see the details of all the calls .”
  • Select Buffer Time . “It will be define as per the Qualified Calls & those will be counted which has duration more than 10 sec.”
  • Select price per call.
  • Select TimeZone.
  • Click on Save and Continue
Step 2- PUBLISHER AND ROUTING

In which you can get a unique tracking number where you or your partners can send calls to them.

  1. Click on ADD PUBLISHER for new or Select an existing Publisher . After Clicking on Add publisher you will see new window on the screen ,there you need to enter your Publisher name, Email ID, Contact, Price per TFNs, Password & Role as publisher .
  1. Select Routing status from drop down list.
  • In-side Route.
  • Outside Route.
  • IVR.

Click on SAVE AND CONTINUE

Step 3- ADD A TARGET
  • Targets are the receiving party of all the calls routed with AlivePBX. The call now starts with a call to a number generated by you or a publisher & ends with the target accepting the answering the call. A Target is defined by an inbound phone number .In this you can Forword your calls to buyers, publisher & Queue or anywhere you would like them to go.

NOTE: Enter the Queue Number if “In-side Route” selected in routing option at Step 2.

NOTE: Enter the Buyer Number and Name if “Out-side Route” selected in routing option at step 2 Or add New Buyer Details or Buyer Number.

  • Select existing IVR if “IVR” selected in routing option at Step 2. IVR routing sense all Interecative Voice call response, they uses nodes and caller propmt to route traffic & routing plan.

Click on SAVE AND CONTINUE.

Step 4- SET IT LIVE

To check your tracking Number Enter the TFN number and call the Publisher, verify that is routed to your Target correctly.

  • Select existing TFNs Or Add New TFNs to route Calls.

    • Click on SAVE AND CONTINUE and Your Campaign is Live.

  • Campaign is created.
