Managing Publisher

“A Publisher is a Partner that is responsible for sending calls to your Campaigns”. Adding a Publisher to a campaign assigns a unique Tracking Number that routes, reports & attributes the calls they generate. Publishers receive a unique Tracking Number for prompting the campaign and routing calls to targets in your routing plan.

To change a Publisher’s detail-

  1. Go to your account, navigate to the Manage Users from the main menu and click on Manage Publisher You can see the list of Publishers select one by using filter option at the right Corner . You will get your publisher. Follow these Steps:

To Manage a publisher follow the below steps.

  • ➢Edit Publisher
    ➢Queue
    ➢Add Buyer
    ➢Settings
    ➢View Reports
    ➢Delete
  • ➢Edit Publisher

    Go to Manage User & select Manage Publisher. To Edit Publisher click on Edit icon in Action bar for respective Publisher, Click on “Edit Publisher” Button & Change the below details.

    For example, it would look like this:

  1. Full Name
  2. Email
  3. Contact
  4. Price Per TFN($)
  5. Password
  6. Confirm Password
  7. Choose Role

If you do not want to Edit then simply click the “Cancel Button”.

  • ➢Queue

    Go to Manage Users & select Manage Publisher. To define unique Publisher’s Queue where you can group the calls & distribute them, then you can use “Add Queue”. Click on  Queue icon in Action bar for respective Publisher Click on “Add Queue” Button & follow the Steps.

    • For example, it would look like this:

  1. Name
  2. Email
  3. Contact
  4. Address($)
  5. Price Per TFN($)
  6. Buffer Time(in seconds)
  7. Password
  8. Confirm Password

NOTE: If you do not want to change then click the “Cancel Button”.

  • ➢Add Buyer

    Go to Manage Users & select Manage Publisher.“A Buyer is a Partner that is receiving your calls”. To Add Buyer click on  Add Buyer icon in Action bar for respective Publisher Click on “Add Buyer“ Button & follow the Steps.

For example, it would look like this:

  1. Buyer Name
  2. Email
  3. Contact
  4. Address($)
  5. Price Per TFN($)
  6. Buffer Time(in seconds)
  7. Password
  8. Confirm Password

NOTE: If you do not want to edit then click the “Cancel Button”.

  • ➢Settings

    Go to Manage Users & select Manage Publisher. To manage Publisher’s settings click on Settings icon in Action bar for respective Publisher & check the requirements.

For example, it would look like this:

  1. Enable Queue monitor: If you are using enable Queue-Monitor then this Publisher will allow to access Queue-monitor screen.

  2. Phone System:

  3. Daily TFN ($): Set the Daily TFN limitations for this Publisher.

  4. Monthly TFN ($): Set the Monthly TFN limitations for this Publisher.

  5. Enable Recording: You want to show the Recordings in Publisher’s CDR reports or not.

  6. Enable inside routing: You want to enable your inside routing during campaign creation or not.

  7. Call Reducer: How many calls publisher can check in CDR reports.

  8. Buyer Limit : Bound to Buyer limit

  9. Display CNUM: You want to display your CID reports or not .

  10. Enable Outside Routing: You want to enable your outside routing during campaign creation or not.

  11. Display wallet: ?

    Go to Manage Users & select Manage Publisher. To delete publisher click on Delete icon in Action bar for respective. You can simply delete the publisher and related details to stop the functions.
